Data Oriented Computer Reports

A collection of reports utilizing python with a data focus

Using Neural Networks to Distinguish Between Images

This report details the use of neural network libraries in python like tensorflow in order to distinguish between multiple pictures of irises with varying degree of differences between each picture. Libraries and languages used: Python, Tensorflow, Numpy.

Identification of Star Clusters

This report details classifying stars through its relative position compared to two other stars. This 3-star tuple is shown to be unique and when standardizing the tuples provide simple identification. Libraries and languages used: Python, SQL, Numpy.

NHTSA Complaints

This report involves aggregating complaint data obtained from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, and in using that data, tries to make a comparasion on the reliability of car models across different brands. Libraries and languages used: Python, JSON, Requests, Numpy.

Unemployment Across US Counties

This report aggregates data of unemployment from each US county across many years and tries to make conclusions of said years and current events with the help of a visual aid map in the form of a choropleth. Libraries and languages used: Python, Glob, XLRD, Numpy.

Aggregation of Name Data

This report focuses on name data of the population of the United States over two centuries ago, in hopes of finding a correlation or reason for the naming scheme of that time.
